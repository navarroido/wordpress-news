🚨 Security
Smart Slider 3 Pro Hacked via Official Update Channel: Act Now
For about six hours on April 7, anyone who updated Smart Slider 3 Pro received a backdoored build, not the real plugin. Attackers breached Nextend's update infrastructure and distributed a remote access toolkit. If you installed version 3.5.1.35, treat your site as fully compromised and upgrade to 3.5.1.36 immediately.
